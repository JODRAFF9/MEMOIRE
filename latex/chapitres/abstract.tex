\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

\textbf{Keywords:} remittances, child multidimensional poverty, Senegal, EHCVM, propensity score matching (PSM), difference-in-differences (DiD).

\bigskip

This thesis examines the impact of migrant remittances on child multidimensional poverty in Senegal, using the Harmonized Household Living Conditions Survey (EHCVM I and II). Child multidimensional poverty is measured through an adapted Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) covering education, health, and living standards dimensions. To identify causal effects, we combine propensity score matching (PSM) with a difference-in-differences (DiD) estimator to address selection bias and time-invariant unobservable heterogeneity.
